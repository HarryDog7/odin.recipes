Onsen Egg

Ingredients

  • 4 cups water
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1cup soya sauce
  • 1cup (cold) water
    •

Instructions

  1. Bring water to boil in a covered pot.
  2. Remove pot from heat, add cold water, and place eggs in pot slowly. Cover.
  3. Remove eggs from pot after 17 minutes.
  4. Place eggs in soya sauce and 1 cup water, store covered for 1 day, (up to 5 days) before consuming.
