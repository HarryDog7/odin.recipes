Chili Chicken

Ingredients

  • 2lb chicken drumsticks
  • 1 bunch cilantro (rinsed and loose chopped)
  • 4 fresh thai chilies (finely sliced)
  • 100g salted butter (melted)
  • 4 cloves garlic (coarsely chopped)
  • 1tbsp chili powder
    •

Instructions

  1. Combine chicken, butter, and chili powder in a large bowl. Toss until chicken is thoroughly coated
  2. Place seasoned drumsticks on baking tray and place in oven at 420f
  3. After 20 min, take out of oven and toss in garlic and sliced chilies, return to oven on tray
  4. Bake for 30 minutes at 420f, and then 10m minutes at 450f
  5. Remove from oven, let cool for 10 minutes, add cilantro as garnish
